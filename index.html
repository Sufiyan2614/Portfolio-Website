How I Work

My design process

01
Discover & Research
I start by understanding your users, business goals, and market context. User interviews, competitive analysis, and empathy mapping establish a solid foundation before any pixels are moved.
02
Define & Strategize
Synthesizing research into actionable insights — user personas, journey maps, problem statements, and clear design principles that guide every decision forward.
03
Wireframe & Prototype
Rapid low-fidelity wireframes evolve into interactive prototypes. I validate concepts early and often, reducing expensive rework during development.
04
Design & Refine
High-fidelity UI design with meticulous attention to typography, color, motion, and accessibility — crafting every detail to be both beautiful and functional.
05
Test & Deliver
Usability testing, iteration, and a clean developer handoff with annotated specs, design tokens, and a Figma file that developers actually love to use.
My Approach

Problems I solve

🔍
Challenge 01
Low user retention & confusing onboarding flows

Products with complex onboarding lose up to 60% of new users in the first week. I redesign these flows with progressive disclosure, contextual tooltips, and clear value milestones that guide users to their "aha moment" faster.

📈 Avg. 40% lift in 7-day retention
⚡
Challenge 02
Inconsistent UI across product teams & platforms

Fragmented design decisions slow dev velocity and damage brand trust. I build scalable Figma design systems — tokens, components, variants — that unify the product language and cut design-to-dev handoff time significantly.

⚡ 50% faster design-to-dev cycles
🎯
Challenge 03
High drop-off on key conversion funnels

Cart abandonment, sign-up friction, and unclear CTAs kill revenue. Through heuristic audits, session analysis, and A/B-tested redesigns, I identify exactly where users drop and redesign those moments with precision.

💰 Avg. 28% increase in conversion rate
📱
Challenge 04
Poor mobile experience on responsive web products

Desktop-first thinking creates broken mobile experiences. I design mobile-first with proper touch targets, thumb-zone optimization, and native platform conventions that feel natural on any screen size.

📱 WCAG AA compliant, 4.8★ app ratings
🧩
Challenge 05
Stakeholder–user needs misalignment in product decisions

Feature bloat and misaligned roadmaps stem from guesswork. I facilitate structured user research, synthesize it into personas and journey maps, and translate data into design principles stakeholders can rally behind.

🧩 Decisions backed by real user data
🏥
Challenge 06
Complex domain UX — healthcare, fintech & SaaS dashboards

High-stakes domains demand clarity under complexity. I specialize in turning dense data, compliance-heavy workflows, and multi-role systems into interfaces that reduce cognitive load without oversimplifying critical information.
